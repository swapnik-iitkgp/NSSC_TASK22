About Us
NSSC saw the light of day in 2011 when it saw a prolific response from all over India in its very first year. In 2012 the fest witnessed an exponential growth in student participation. And from then, NSSC grew to both in popularity and diversity. The fest primarily focuses on giving hand on experience to the students in solving real time technological problems faced in exploring the far reaches of Space and also conducts workshops to guide the present generation.
